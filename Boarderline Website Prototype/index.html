Boarderline Ski and Snowboard Club

James Madison University

Boarderline is the official ski and snowboard club at James Madison Univeristy. With over 350 members, we are the largest sports club on campus and the second largest organization on campus. We are a co-ed community of the raddest people on campus that come together to share a love for skiing and snowboarding. We aren't only found shredding on the slopes. In the off AND on season, we are seen in our community volunteering and fundraising, hosting social events with other organizations on campus, and fostering community within our own club through our Health and Wellness events.
